How to Use This Tool

  • To generate a QR code: Enter any text or URL in the generator field, customize the size and colors if needed, then click "Generate QR Code".
  • To decode a QR code: Click the upload area and select an image containing a QR code, or drag and drop the image onto the area. Then click "Decode QR Code".
  • Supported image formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, and WebP.
  • Privacy note: All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.
